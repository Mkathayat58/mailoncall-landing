🚀 Getting Started

Currently MailOnCall is built exclusively for Microsoft Outlook — including personal Outlook accounts (outlook.com, hotmail.com) and business Microsoft 365 accounts. Gmail support is not available at this time. We integrate directly via the Microsoft Graph API, which provides secure, real-time email monitoring.

Setting up takes under 5 minutes:

  1. Sign up at app.mailoncall.net
  2. Connect your Outlook account via Microsoft OAuth
  3. Go to Alert Rules and click Create rule
  4. Set your conditions — sender email, subject keywords, etc.
  5. Enter your phone number and save
  6. Use the Test button to verify it works

No downloads or installations required. MailOnCall is entirely web-based — access your dashboard from any browser at app.mailoncall.net. Calls are delivered to your existing phone number, so no app is needed on your phone either.

📞 How It Works

MailOnCall uses Microsoft Graph API webhooks to detect emails in real time — typically within 10 seconds of the email arriving in your inbox. Once detected, Twilio places the call immediately. Total time from email arrival to your phone ringing is usually under 30 seconds.

That depends on your plan:

  • Starter: One call attempt, no retries
  • Pro: Up to 3 retry attempts at 5-minute intervals
  • Team/Business: Custom retry intervals + escalation to secondary and tertiary contacts

If the call goes to voicemail, MailOnCall detects this automatically and treats it as a missed call — retries will still fire if enabled.

The call plays an automated voice message that includes the sender's email address and the subject line of the email. The message uses Amazon Polly's natural-sounding text-to-speech voice so it's clear and easy to understand.

⚙️ Alert Rules

You can filter emails by:

  • Sender email — alert only when email is from a specific address
  • Sender domain — alert for any email from @supplier.com
  • Subject keywords — alert when subject contains "URGENT" or "Invoice"
  • Body keywords — alert when body contains specific phrases

Combine multiple conditions using AND or OR logic for precise filtering.

It depends on your plan:

  • Starter (Free): Up to 3 alert rules
  • Pro, Team, Business: Unlimited alert rules

Yes — every rule has a toggle switch in the dashboard. Turn it off to pause monitoring for that rule, and turn it back on whenever you're ready. Paused rules don't fire alerts but count toward your plan limit.

Yes — every alert rule has a Test button (▷) in the dashboard. Clicking it sends a test call and SMS to your configured number immediately, so you can verify everything works before relying on it for real alerts.

🔒 Security & Privacy

No — MailOnCall does not store your email content. When a matching email is detected, we read only the sender address and subject line to generate the voice message. This data is used in real time and not persisted. We never read, store or process the body of your emails beyond checking for keyword matches.

MailOnCall uses Microsoft OAuth 2.0 — the same secure standard used by thousands of business applications. You log in with your Microsoft account and grant specific read-only permissions. Your password is never shared with or stored by MailOnCall. You can revoke access at any time from your Microsoft account settings.
