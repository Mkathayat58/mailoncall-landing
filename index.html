How it works

Three steps.
Zero missed emails.

Set up in under 5 minutes. Works silently in the background, 24/7.

01

Connect your Outlook

Sign in with your Microsoft account. MailOnCall connects securely via Microsoft Graph — no passwords stored, ever.

02

Set your alert rules

Define what matters — by sender, subject keywords, or urgency flags. Your rules, your priorities.

03

Get the call

The moment a matching email arrives, your phone rings. A clear voice reads you the sender, subject, and preview. Instantly.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about MailOnCall.

Getting Started

Currently MailOnCall works exclusively with Microsoft Outlook — including personal accounts (outlook.com, hotmail.com) and business Microsoft 365 accounts. We integrate via the Microsoft Graph API for real-time monitoring.

Setup takes under 5 minutes: sign up → connect Outlook via Microsoft login → create a rule with your conditions → enter your phone number → hit Test. That's it.

Nothing to install. MailOnCall is entirely web-based at app.mailoncall.net. Calls go to your existing phone number — no app needed on your mobile.

Yes — every rule has a Test ▷ button that fires a real call and SMS to your configured number immediately so you can verify everything works.

Alert Rules

Filter by sender email, sender domain, subject keywords, or body keywords. Combine conditions with AND or OR logic for precise targeting.

Starter: up to 3 rules. Pro, Team, Business: unlimited rules.

Yes — every rule has a toggle. Turn it off to pause, back on to resume. Paused rules don't fire alerts.

Plans & Billing

Yes — completely free, no time limit, no credit card required. 1 mailbox and up to 3 alert rules at no cost.

Yes — no lock-in, no cancellation fees. Cancel from Settings → Billing. Your plan stays active until the billing period ends, then drops to Starter.

Yes — all prices are in AUD. GST is not included. International customers are charged in AUD at their bank's exchange rate.

We don't refund partial months. If a technical issue prevented you from using the service, email support@mailoncall.net and we'll review your case.

How It Works

Microsoft Graph API webhooks detect emails in real time — typically within 10 seconds. Twilio then places the call immediately. Phone to ring: usually under 30 seconds total.

Starter: one attempt, no retries. Pro: up to 3 retries at 5-min intervals. Team/Business: custom intervals + escalation to secondary and tertiary contacts. Voicemail is detected automatically.

An automated voice reads the sender's email address and the subject line. Uses Amazon Polly's natural-sounding voice — clear and easy to understand.

Yes — the SMS alert includes a one-tap Acknowledge link. Tapping it stops all further retries instantly. Available on Pro, Team and Business plans.

Security & Privacy

No. We read only the sender and subject to generate the voice message — in real time, not stored. We never store email body content.

Via Microsoft OAuth 2.0 — your password is never shared or stored. You grant read-only permissions and can revoke access anytime from your Microsoft account.
