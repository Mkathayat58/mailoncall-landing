For IT Teams

One shared mailbox.
Your whole team on-call.

Connect a shared Outlook mailbox and automatically call whoever is on the roster — no manual checking, no missed alerts.

👥

Team roster management

Set up who is on-call for each day and time slot. The system automatically calls the right person at the right time.

📬

Shared mailbox support

Connect a shared Outlook mailbox like oncall@company.com. One connection covers your entire team.

🔺

3-level escalation

Primary doesn't answer? Automatically escalates to secondary, then tertiary. Nobody gets left behind.

🔄

Automatic retry

Retries up to 3 times before escalating. Configurable retry count per escalation level.

✅

SMS acknowledgement

On-call staff receive an SMS with a one-click acknowledge link. Stops retries instantly when tapped.

📋

Full audit trail

Every alert, call, and acknowledgement logged with timestamps. Compliance-ready reporting.
