Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use MailOnCall:

  • Account information (name, email address, password hash)
  • Phone numbers you provide for call alerts
  • Alert rules you create (sender filters, keywords)
  • Call and notification history
  • Microsoft OAuth tokens (encrypted, for Outlook access)
  • Billing information (processed securely by Stripe)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not store the content of your emails. We only access email metadata (sender, subject) to match your alert rules. Email content is never stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the MailOnCall service
  • To send automated phone calls when your rules are triggered
  • To send service emails (receipts, alerts, account notices)
  • To improve and maintain the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Render (Singapore region). We implement industry-standard security measures including bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication, TLS encryption, and HMAC API key verification.

5. Microsoft Data

When you connect your Outlook account, we receive OAuth tokens from Microsoft to access your inbox. These tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We only read email metadata necessary to match your alert rules. You can disconnect your Outlook account at any time from your dashboard.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Twilio — phone call delivery
  • Microsoft Graph API — Outlook integration
  • Resend — transactional emails
  • Render — hosting and database

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at admin@mailoncall.net to exercise these rights.

9. Cookies

We use session storage and local storage for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at admin@mailoncall.net
